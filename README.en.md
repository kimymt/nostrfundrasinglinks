## Nostr Grants

### OpenSats Nostr Fund
**URL:** [opensats.org/projects/nostr](https://opensats.org/projects/nostr)

Overview: A fund that supports the development and improvement of the *Nostr protocol and related* services. It is used to support projects that make Nostr more accessible, secure, decentralized, and easy to use. It also helps fund projects that educate people about Nostr and its possibilities.

Funding: Amounts are denominated in US dollars and sent as sats at the exchange rate at the time of each monthly disbursement.

> **Note:** The selection criteria are not explicitly stated on the site, but it is presumed that they are essentially the General Fund's criteria below with "Bitcoin" replaced by "Nostr protocol." Please confirm with the operators yourself before applying.

### OpenSats General Fund
**URL:** [opensats.org/projects/general_fund](https://opensats.org/projects/general_fund)

Overview: A fund that supports *Bitcoin-related* open-source projects. Eligible projects must meet the following criteria:
1. Be related to Bitcoin
2. Be free and open source
3. Be polished and have the potential to make a significant impact on the Bitcoin ecosystem

OpenSats reviews every funding application and considers the following factors when deciding on funding:
1. Alignment with the OpenSats mission
2. Feasibility and impact on the ecosystem
3. Strength of leadership and team
4. Need for funding

Funding: Amounts are denominated in US dollars and sent as sats at the exchange rate at the time of each monthly disbursement.

### Bitcoin Adoption Impact Fund
**URL:** [geyser.fund/impact-funds](https://geyser.fund/impact-funds)

Overview: A fund aimed at realizing a stronger and more widely used Bitcoin. It focuses on Bitcoin adoption and awareness in regions Wall Street does not serve.

**Fund characteristics:**

- **Local First**
  Local businesses are the foundation of a strong economy. The fund provides financial support to people starting or growing local businesses.

- **Trusted Network**
  Trustless money enables a healthier trust-based society. Bitcoin makes new human relationships and trust networks possible. The fund leverages these networks to fund local projects.

- **Circular Economy**
  Use it and circulate it. The new global Bitcoin economy is being built one Bitcoin transaction at a time. Start local and grow globally.

Funding: Sent as sats upon selection.
